How it works

From kickoff to
match point

  1. 1

    Choose your format

    Best of 1, 3 or 5 sets. Golden Point or ventajas at deuce. Tie-break and Super Tie-break on or off. Set your padel format once, then play.

  2. 2

    Pick who serves first

    Tap your side on the watch. PadelDeck tracks serve rotation automatically every game — and every two points in a tie-break.

  3. 3

    Tap to score

    Your half of the screen for your team, the other half for the opponents. One tap per point — no buttons to find, no menus to navigate.

  4. 4

    Review your match

    After the last point, your match is saved on the watch automatically. Set breakdown, total points, duration and format — all on your wrist.

FAQ

Everything you
need to know

Which Amazfit watches does PadelDeck support?
PadelDeck runs on the Amazfit Balance with Zepp OS 3.26 or later. Support for additional Zepp OS 3 devices is planned for future releases.
Is PadelDeck free?
Yes — completely free. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no account needed to track and review your matches.
Do I need my phone while playing?
No. PadelDeck works fully offline on your Amazfit Balance. Your phone can stay in your bag. Scores, match history and settings are all managed directly on the watch.
Which padel scoring formats are supported?
PadelDeck supports best of 1, 3 or 5 sets; ventajas (classic advantage) or Golden Point at deuce; configurable tie-break (first to 7); and Super Tie-break (first to 10) as the deciding set.
What happens if I tap the wrong side?
Hit undo directly on the score screen. PadelDeck saves a full snapshot before every point, so you can reverse up to 20 consecutive points without losing any match data.
